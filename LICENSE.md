## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

- ❌ **Calamari Executor won't launch on Windows**
  Heuristic-based antivirus tools sometimes quarantine the executable at startup.
  **Solution:** Add the entire `C:\Program Files\CalamariExecutor` directory to your antivirus exclusion list, then re-download and install.

- ❌ **Injection fails with "timeout" error**
  This typically occurs when Roblox has not fully loaded before Calamari Executor attempts injection.
  **Solution:** Wait until you are fully loaded into the game world, then click **Execute** again. Increasing `injection_delay` to `500ms` in the config can also help.

- ❌ **Calamari Executor Ios crashes on launch**
  The sideloaded `.ipa` may have an expired signing certificate.
  **Solution:** Re-sign the Calamari Executor Ios `.ipa` using your sideloading tool and reinstall it on your device.

- ❌ **Scripts not appearing in Script Hub**
  A connectivity issue may be preventing the hub from fetching the latest script database.
  **Solution:** Check your internet connection, then restart Calamari Executor. If the issue persists, clear the cache at `~/CalamariExecutor/cache`.

- ❌ **macOS Gatekeeper blocks the app**
  Apple blocks unsigned applications by default on macOS.
  **Solution:** Right-click the app and select **Open**, then approve it in `System Preferences > Privacy & Security`.

- ❌ **High CPU usage during execution**
  Some complex scripts with heavy loops can spike processor usage.
  **Solution:** Optimize your script by reducing loop frequency, or enable `sandbox_scripts` in the Calamari Executor config to limit resource allocation.
